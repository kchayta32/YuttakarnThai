# RTS: Yutthakan Thailand - Campaign Design Sheets

## Campaign 1: Burmese–Siamese War (1547–1549)
**Title:** The War of the White Elephants (สงครามช้างเผือก) / First Siege
**Briefing:** King Tabinshwehti of Toungoo invades Siam through the Three Pagodas Pass. Queen Suriyothai rides into battle to save her husband. This campaign serves as the tutorial for basic movement and melee combat.
**Map:** Kanchanaburi plains -> Ayutthaya walls.
**Unique Mechanics:**
*   **Elephant Duel:** Hero units (Kings/Queens) on elephants have special duel mechanics.
*   **River Transport:** Use barges to move troops across the Chao Phraya.
**Unit Roster:**
*   **Siam:** Swordsman, Pikeman, Archer, War Elephant, Royal Barge.
*   **Burma:** Mercenary Matchlock (Portuguese), War Elephant, Lancer, Siege Tower.
**Objectives:**
1.  Defend the river crossing (Hold for 5 min).
2.  Escort the Royal Barge to the capital.
3.  Defeat the Vanguard Commander.
**Events:** Queen Suriyothai's Sacrifice (Scripted Cutscene when King's HP < 30%).

## Campaign 2: Tha Din Daeng Campaign (1786)
**Title:** The Battle of Tha Din Daeng (สงครามท่าดินแดง)
**Briefing:** King Rama I leads the Rattanakosin army to intercept Bodawpaya's massive invasion force. This is a battle of logistics and jungle warfare.
**Map:** Dense jungle valleys in Kanchanaburi. Narrow paths.
**Unique Mechanics:**
*   **Supply Lines:** Troops lose morale quickly if too far from Supply Depots.
*   **Ambush:** Siamese units get +50% first-strike damage when attacking from "Hidden" state in forests.
**Objectives:**
1.  Locate enemy supply caches.
2.  Destroy the 3 forward operating bases.
**Unit Roster:**
*   **Siam:** Jungle Scout, Musketeer, Heavy Elephant, Mortar Team.
*   **Burma:** Conscript Infantry, Heavy Cannon, Elite Guard.

## Campaign 3: Franco-Siamese War (1893)
**Title:** The Paknam Incident (วิกฤตการณ์ปากน้ำ)
**Briefing:** French gunboats force their way up the Chao Phraya River. Siam must defend the capital while navigating a delicate diplomatic crisis.
**Map:** Chao Phraya River mouth, Phra Chulachomklao Fort.
**Unique Mechanics:**
*   **Diplomatic Timer:** Do not sink the "Diplomatic Envoy" ship, or you lose the game immediately. Only engage military vessels.
*   **Fort Batteries:** Control stationary heavy coastal guns.
**Objectives:**
1.  Damage the French flagship 'Inconstant' to 50% HP (Force retreat).
2.  Prevent French marines from landing at the Palace.
**Unit Roster:**
*   **Siam:** Armstrong Gun (Stationary), Mauser Rifleman, Steam Gunboat.
*   **France:** Ironclad Cruiser, Marine Infantry, Foreign Legion.

## Campaign 4: World War I (1918)
**Title:** The Expeditionary Force (กองกำลังทหารอาสา)
**Briefing:** The Siamese Expeditionary Force arrives in France. They must adapt to industrial warfare, gas attacks, and trench raids.
**Map:** Champagne region, France. Muddy trenches, No Man's Land.
**Unique Mechanics:**
*   **Gas Attack:** Random events where Mustard Gas covers areas. Units must use "Mask Up" ability.
*   **Artillery Support:** Off-map artillery cooldown ability.
**Objectives:**
1.  Hold the trench line against 3 German waves.
2.  Capture the enemy pillbox.
**Unit Roster:**
*   **Siam:** Trench Raider, Transport Driver, Medic.
*   **Germany:** Stormtrooper, Machine Gun Nest, Biplane Bomber.

## Campaign 5: Boworadet Rebellion (1933)
**Title:** The Battle for the Constitution (กบฏบวรเดช)
**Briefing:** Prince Boworadet leads troops from Korat to bangkok to challenge the new government. It is a civil war fighting for political ideology.
**Map:** Railway line from Don Mueang to Lak Si.
**Unique Mechanics:**
*   **Armored Train:** A mobile fortress that moves along rails. Control the switches to change its path.
*   **Propaganda:** Pamphlet drops convert neutral/wavering units.
**Objectives:**
1.  Derail or Destroy the Rebel Armored Train.
2.  Secure the Don Mueang Airfield.
**Unit Roster:**
*   **Govt:** AA Gun, Militia, Armored Car.
*   **Rebels:** Royalist Guard, Field Gun, Armored Train.

## Campaign 6: Franco-Thai War (1940–1941)
**Title:** Battle of Yuwonkela (ยุทธนาวีเกาะช้าง / Indochina borders)
**Briefing:** Thai forces advance into French Indochina to reclaim lost territories. The Royal Thai Air Force battles Vichy French aircraft.
**Map:** Cambodian border jungles and Gulf of Thailand (Naval).
**Unique Mechanics:**
*   **Air Superiority:** Control airfields to launch airstrikes.
*   **Combined Arms:** Tanks get bonuses when near Infantry.
**Objectives:**
1.  Capture the border town of Poipet.
2.  Defend the fleet at Koh Chang (Survival timer).
**Unit Roster:**
*   **Siam:** Vickers Tank, Hawk III Fighter, Torpedo Boat.
*   **Vichy France:** Morane Fighter, Light Tank, Colonial Infantry.

## Campaign 7: Japanese Invasion (1941)
**Title:** The Heroes of Ao Manao (วีรชนอ่าวมะนาว)
**Briefing:** December 8, 1941. The Imperial Japanese Army lands at Prachuap Khiri Khan. A small Air Wing and police force must hold the beach.
**Map:** Ao Manao Bay (Beach landing -> Airfield defense).
**Unique Mechanics:**
*   **Ammo Scarcity:** Units have limited ammo. Must resupply at HQ.
*   **Overwhelming Odds:** Enemy spawns are infinite. Objective is "Survive X Hours" until ceasefire order.
**Objectives:**
1.  Defend the Command Building for 20 minutes.
2.  Keep the radio tower operational.
**Unit Roster:**
*   **Siam:** Police Cadet, Airman Ground Crew, Heavy MG.
*   **Japan:** Imperial Marine, Type 95 Ha-Go Tank, Zero Fighter (Strafing run).

## Campaign 8: Korean War (1950–1953)
**Title:** Little Tigers of Pork Chop Hill (พยัคฆ์น้อย)
**Briefing:** The 21st Royal Thai Regiment joins the UN forces. They must defend strategic hills against waves of Chinese/North Korean forces in freezing winter.
**Map:** Pork Chop Hill. Steep elevation, snow.
**Unique Mechanics:**
*   **Freezing:** Units away from bunkers lose HP slowly.
*   **Close Quarters:** "Hand-to-Hand" bonus for Thai troops reflecting their reputation for bravery in melee trenches.
**Objectives:**
1.  Repel 5 waves of night attacks.
2.  Escort wounded to the the extract point.
**Unit Roster:**
*   **Thailand/UN:** M1 Garand Infantry, Bazooka Team, M24 Chaffee.
*   **Opfor:** Human Wave Infantry, T-34 Tank, Mortar Squad.

## Campaign 9: Near Future (2025)
**Title:** Operation Naga Protect (ปฏิบัติการนาคราช)
**Briefing:** A fictional border dispute escalates. A rogue faction threatens regional stability. The Royal Thai Army deploys a modern peace-enforcement task force.
**Map:** Urban city center + Rural border crossing.
**Unique Mechanics:**
*   **Drone Warfare:** Use Drones to scout and strike.
*   **ROE (Rules of Engagement):** Do not kill Civilians. Collateral damage reduces "Legitimacy" score.
*   **Main Battle Tank:** Use the Oplot/VT-4 equivalent.
**Objectives:**
1.  Neutralize insurgent MLRS sites.
2.  Secure the civilian evacuation corridor.
**Unit Roster:**
*   **RTA 2025:** Modern Infantry (Assault Rifle), DTI-1 Guided Missile, UAV, Stryker APC.
*   **Insurgents:** Technical (Pickup+MG), IED Planter, Hacker (Disables vehicles).
